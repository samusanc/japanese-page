How to Play

  • Review 5 cards before starting the speed matching challenge.
  • Match the upper Spanish (Red) card with its lower Japanese (Green) pair.
  • Both card decks are at the top, dealt directly by the cyber opponent.
  • You have 3 seconds per card to clear the entire board (no timer reset on single matches).
  • Clearing the board unlocks a new review session to expand your deck (+5 pairs).
  • Spaced repetition appearance weights prioritize cards you missed, skipped, or left on the table!
